SCÖRE

KVLT SCÖRE is an interactive web app that tracks the amount of Heavy Metal bands globally. It is powered by the Echo Nest API, an online music database. A user enters a country and metal sub genre of their choosing into the search fields and data is returned in the form of a KVLT RATING. KVLT RATINGS are based on the amount of recorded bands in the Echo Nest API for a given country, depending upon the amount of bands, the KVLT SCÖRE rates how KVLT a country is.
